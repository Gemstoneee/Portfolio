Research and Development Department

During the first three weeks of our internship, we were given video tutorials about PHP and Laravel. We watched the tutorials and practiced the given exercises included. While learning these new languages, the programmers of our department never failed to help us when we have questions. They guided us on how to make the project that were assigned to us.

Summary of Weekly Activities

  • Research

    Inorder for us to make our project, we studied new languages that would be used for our system. Our department provided us the learning materials for us to study. I was able to learn new languages such as PHP, Lavarel, CSS and also how to use Git.

  • Develop

    After learning the things that are needed for us to make our system. We started doing the modules for our project. First off, we design the user interface then work on the database and the classes for each module.

  • Rest

    Every month the company has its Rest and Recreation. This May they organized coreDevlympics. We had a team building at Family Park, Talamaban, together will all employees and us interns.

Things learned during OJT

I have learned a lot of things during my internship. I was introduced to a new programming language, PHP and a PHP framework Laravel. Even though we haven't use the languages thought in school, I can still say that the knowledge that I have learned in school was put to good use and was enhance. In addition to that, this internship gives me an insight of what the industry would be like. I have learned how to work independently and figure things out on my own.

Ways to Improve the OJT Program

The school should establish partnership with more Information Technology companies so that we students can narrow down the companies to apply. Also, the processing of the OIR documents should be improve. Going back and forth from the office to school is tiring and time consuming.
